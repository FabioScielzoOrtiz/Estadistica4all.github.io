What is Estadistica4all ?



Estadistica4all is a dissemination blog about statistics, machine learning and data science. Our purpose is to provide useful material for people who are starting out in this world, as well as for people who already have some experience.

Our philosophy is to treat topics from a technical perspective, having a certain mathematical formalism when necessary, and good code when required.
